Aesthetic QR code - examples

Fig. 9(a):
λ=0 (extreme)

Transition cost factor λ=0
Error-correction level: Low
Can correct up to 10 codeword errors

Due to no transition cost, this video QR code changes every frame.

Fig. 9(b):
λ=0.5

Transition cost factor λ=0.5
Error-correction level: Low
Can correct up to 10 codeword errors

Using this more "balanced" value of λ, this video QR code changes only when many frames between QR code and logo are significantly disaligned such that they outweighed the transition cost.

Fig. 9(c):
λ=100 (extreme)

Transition cost factor λ=100
Error-correction level: Low
Can correct up to 10 codeword errors

Due to very large transition cost, this video QR code does not do transitions, and instead uses the most representative QR code for across all frames.

Fig. 9(d):
λ=0.1 (with erasure)

Transition cost factor λ=0.1
Error-correction level: High
With 22 codeword erasure chosen greedily
Can correct up to 10 codeword errors

For each frame, greedily choose 22 codewords to erase, then apply our modified RS code padding-parity bit selection algorithm.
